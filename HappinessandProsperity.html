In Basic Human Aspiration.

Happiness

Happiness is the state of mental and emotional well-being. It is the joy found in relationships, self-acceptance, health, and personal fulfillment.

Balance

A true sense of well-being comes from achieving harmony between happiness and prosperity. Both are essential for a fulfilling life.

Prosperity

Prosperity is the experience of growth, security, and wealth. It includes financial success, but also the ability to give back and contribute to the community.
